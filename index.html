The Build Process

01

The Ventilation Gap

We engineer a critical 3-6" air channel between the sauna roof and the deck joists. This prevents condensation buildup and protects your home's structural integrity from the 190°F+ temperatures below.

  • Passive airflow system
  • Moisture-wicking membranes
02

Precision Heating

The HUUM Hive provides a high-density stone capacity for soft, long-lasting steam (Leil). Paired with smart controls, you can pre-heat the sanctuary from your phone before you even arrive home.

INSPECTION PARTNER Metro West Inspections

Structural Permitting

Under-deck enclosures are categorized as "accessory structures." We handle all architectural drawings and site plans required by Wayzata city hall.

Electrical Variance

Saunas require specific load calculations. We ensure your existing panel can support the 9kW+ draw of the Hive heater.

Egress & Safety

Tempered glass doors must swing outward and require no latching mechanism for emergency exit compliance.
